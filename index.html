About Kuznix

Kuznix GNU/Linux is a rolling-release distribution based on Arch Linux. This is a fresh restart of Kuznix, moving away from LFS. Development may not have started yet, but work is planned for late October or early November 2025.

Upcoming Changes

  • Introduction of Kuznix’s own repositories, replacing AUR (removal planned for 2026–2027)
  • Optimized package manager and dependency handling
  • Rolling ISO releases and installer updates
  • Enhanced gaming and multimedia support
